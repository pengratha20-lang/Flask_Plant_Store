Plant Care Made Easy

Learn how to keep your plants healthy and thriving with our expert tips

Watering Guide

Learn the right watering techniques for different plant types. Overwatering is one of the most common mistakes.

Watering Tips:
  • Check soil moisture before watering - stick your finger 1-2 inches deep
  • Water thoroughly until water drains from the bottom
  • Most plants prefer morning watering
  • Use room temperature water
  • Different seasons require different watering frequencies
Light Requirements

Understand how much light your plants need and how to position them for optimal growth and health.

Light Guidelines:
  • Bright Direct: 6+ hours of direct sunlight (succulents, herbs)
  • Bright Indirect: Bright but filtered light (most houseplants)
  • Medium Light: East/west facing windows (pothos, snake plants)
  • Low Light: North facing windows or artificial light
  • Rotate plants weekly for even growth
Fertilizing Tips

Discover the best fertilizing practices to keep your plants nutritionally balanced and thriving.

Fertilizing Schedule:
  • Spring/Summer: Fertilize every 2-4 weeks (growing season)
  • Fall/Winter: Reduce to monthly or skip entirely
  • Use diluted liquid fertilizer (half strength)
  • Always water before fertilizing to prevent root burn
  • Look for yellowing leaves as signs of over-fertilization
